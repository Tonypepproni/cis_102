About the Competition

The Embedded Capture the Flag (eCTF) Competition, run by MITRE, is a challenge focused on designing and attacking secure embedded systems. Participants first work in teams to create a secure system for an embedded device based on specific requirements. In the second phase, teams analyze and attack each otherâ€™s systems, testing the security of their designs. This competition offers a hands-on experience in both building and breaking embedded security systems, with real physical devices involved.

In the 2025 eCTF, teams will design and implement a Satellite TV system. The system must securely encode and decode satellite TV data streams while protecting against unauthorized access to protected channels.
